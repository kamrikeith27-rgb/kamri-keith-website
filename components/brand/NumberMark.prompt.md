The jersey-number brand device — Kamri's stand-in mark (no logo exists). Use it as an avatar, favicon-style badge, or hero accent.

```jsx
<NumberMark value="5" tone="royal" size={120} />
<NumberMark value="K5" shape="plate" tone="ink" />
<NumberMark value="5" shape="shield" tone="accent" />
```

Shapes: `circle | shield | plate`. Tones: `royal | ink | accent | outline | chalk`. Keep it typographic — never redraw as a logo.
